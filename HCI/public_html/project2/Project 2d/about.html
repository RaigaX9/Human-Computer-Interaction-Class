About Us

Botinnovations.com is a non-profit organization where users can learn about robotics or express their ideas of making their own. Our goal is to inspire those that wants to make a career of building robots of their own that will benefit everyone who uses them. As technology continues to evolve, so does robotics and we are finding new and better ways of making them more efficient and reliable then the previous ones by understanding what kinds of functionality we need for them.

There is no cost to be part of this organization and we exist as long as there are those that are interested in robotics. We provide information such as videos, pictures, and news articles for users to understand the kinds of robots that are out there in the world. We also have a submission page where anyone can submit their own ideas of the robots they created or envision as to what they want to see being made. So check on each of the tabs and explore the world of robotics. May the future rest upon you.
