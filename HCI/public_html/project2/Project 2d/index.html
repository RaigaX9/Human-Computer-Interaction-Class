Welcome to BotInnovations.com!

BotInnovations is a non-profit organzation for those who are interested in learning about robotics engineering or want to submit ideas of their own robots as well. This website shows everything that relates to with robotics that is built and being used around the world to gain a better understanding as to how they will work.
