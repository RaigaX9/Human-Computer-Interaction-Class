Project Proposal by Nishad Shah

Name of the website: asem.org

Goals and Objective For The Website:

For this project, I will create a museum website called the Atlanta South Eastern Museum (ASEM). This is where anyone will learn about the history of Georgia and North Florida from 1500 to 1821. It will cover from the discovery of Georgia and Florida to the time where Spain surrendered Florida to the United States.

For the website, it will consist of several pages such as the home page, about us, gallery, information, and the contacts page. Each of these pages will provide their own information for what these sections will include. For example, the "About Us" page will give a brief description of what the website and the museum is about. Another example is the "Gallery" page, which will consist of pictures of drawings, documents, and models that are in the museum. Another is the "Information" page where it will provide some details about the history of Georgia and Florida. The website's main purpose is to provide some historic information of Georgia and Florida from 1500 to 1821 for those that are interested in learning about them.

Demographics:

Age The range is from the age of 10 or above.
Sex Both males and females are welcome to come visit the website
Education High School (or GED) and above.
Computer Literacy Need to have at least intermediate skills to use this website when it comes to understanding how to use the internet.
Interests For those that is interested in history especially for those that either wants to know the history of Georgia and Florida. Other interests can be for those that wants to do research to find out more about them.

What are the users trying to learn and/or accomplish at the web site?

The users will learn about the history of Georgia and Florida from 1500 to 1821. This website is an opportunity to not only gain knowledge as to what happened during that time, but they can learn how it's affecting the present time as well as how it will affect in the future. It’s going to give them vast knowledge in terms of how they discovered both of these states and why certain battles occured during that time. This is especially good for those that doesn't know the history during that period and they can always come visit the website to get basic information about them. Also, if users wants to do some kind of historic research, then they can use this website as their main source for it. That way, the ASEM will not only provide information at their museum physically, but on online as well and that way, this can become the most notable museum that is out there in Atlanta.

What tasks do they have and what actions will they take to accomplish these?

They can go into any sections such as the “About Us”, “Gallery”, “Information”, and “Contacts” where they can navigate the types of history that are there. Here is how this is set up:

  • About Us Section: Users will click on this section to understand what the website is about.

  • Gallery Section: Users will see various pictures of models, documents, and other images posted along with a small description about what they are.

  • Information Section: Users will read information of what happened in each period of both Georgia and Florida as well as some of the major figures like Oglethorpe, for example, who discovered Georgia.

  • Contacts Section: Users can either call or email to customer service is they have any questions or concerns about the website. There will also be a feedback form for users to give their thoughts about the museum and/or for the website.

The users can take action for accomplishing their own desires to understand the history of Southern Eastern U.S. by going into each of these sections to navigate themselves to different pages. There, they will be in a user-friendly environment where they can enjoy the learning the history of Georgia and Florida in which they will get in a long term.
